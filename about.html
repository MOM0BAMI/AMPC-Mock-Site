About AMPC

The African Motion Picture Company (AMPC) is a BBEE Level 2 creative production house with 26 years of experience. We specialize in producing dynamic and award-winning corporate films, TV commercials, live events, and multimedia content for top brands across South Africa.

Our comprehensive services cover every aspect of production—from conceptualization and scriptwriting to filming, post-production, and digital content creation. With a talented team of professionals, we craft tailored video solutions that effectively communicate our clients' messages.

AMPC takes pride in delivering personal, intimate service along with innovative and high-quality work. We support skills development by employing previously disadvantaged individuals and providing opportunities for young Black filmmakers.

Our Mission

At AMPC, we believe in the power of storytelling. Our mission is to deliver high-quality, visually stunning projects that not only capture the essence of our clients' brands but also leave a lasting impact on their audiences.

From pre-production to post, we are hands-on in every step of the process, ensuring that each project is treated with the care and dedication it deserves.
