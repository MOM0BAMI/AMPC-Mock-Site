About AMPC

The African Motion Picture Company (AMPC) is a BBEE Level 2 creative production house with over 26 years of experience. From our inception, we have been passionate about crafting dynamic and award-winning content that inspires, informs, and captivates audiences. Whether it's corporate films, TV commercials, live events, or multimedia projects, AMPC is dedicated to excellence and innovation.

We serve some of the biggest brands across South Africa, offering a seamless, end-to-end production service. Our expertise spans conceptualization, scripting, filming, post-production, and digital content creation, ensuring every project is completed with precision and creativity. We thrive on bringing our clients' visions to life while maintaining a personalized and hands-on approach.
